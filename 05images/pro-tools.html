Pro Tools

Our professional tools are built to support technicians in delivering precise, efficient, and consistent results. From shaping and refining to building structure and adding shine, these tools make services smoother and help maintain the highest standards of safety and quality for every client.

Best Practices for Professionals:

E-Files

Choose balanced handpieces and high-quality bits to reduce vibration and improve finish quality. Keep speed appropriate to the task and protect the nail plate.

Gels

Match viscosity to the service. Self-leveling builders speed overlays, while thicker products offer more control for extensions. Always follow manufacturer cure times.

Review safety data sheets for the brands you use and verify storage and handling recommendations. See Akzentz SDS resources for examples.

Prep and Finish

Safe application starts with proper prep and ends with controlled removal. For general nail product and salon safety guidance, the U.S. Food and Drug Administration provides an overview at FDA Nail Care Products.

Tool Care List

  • Clean bits between clients and replace when worn
  • Wipe lamps and check output per manufacturer guidance
  • Store gels away from direct light and heat
